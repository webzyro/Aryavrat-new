SOLAR STREET LIGHT

Solar Street Light is a raised light that uses photovoltaic technology to convert the sunlight into direct electric current through solar cells powering LED lamps to work throughout the night. Being independent of utility grid solar street lights minimized the operational cost.

Benefits

Sourse Of Energy:

Sun is the biggest source of energy, so no requirement for electricity.

Easy To Use :

Very easy to install and can easily be carried to remote areas.

Less Maintaince:

It is simple to operate and requires less maintenance as compared to other lights.

Eco Friendly:

And of course, Solar Street Lights are eco friendly and eliminates your carbon footprints contribution.
